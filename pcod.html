PCOD or Polycystic Ovary Syndrome Profile Blood is a test that assesses the functions and levels of various hormones in your body. The Polycystic Ovarian Syndrome (PCOS) is found in 5-10% of women between the age of 15 and 44 who are in their reproductive stage. Most women find out about their PCOS in their 20s and 30s when they have trouble conceiving.
